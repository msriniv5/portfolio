DAISY CHAIN

An app that connects preschool and parents

An app was designed as part of an academic project to connect preschool students and parents. To give daily updates and a live video to know the student's activity was also integrated in the design. Proto.io was the tool used for designing the screens

  1. To implement this app, many needfinding techniques were used such as Interviews, questionnaires and survey. Personas were also created for three people to find out the problem they face keeping their busy schedule in mind and solutions were implemented through this app.
  2. The questionnaires, survey results are shown below. A google form was created and sent to many people and the results were tabulated and hence the major problems were noticed.
  3. The wizard of Oz technique was implemented and a phone bot was created in order to answer the questions the parents have regarding the school and the activities of their children.

The questions addressed to the users based on results from the personas are as shown. The results of the same are shown below

Based on the responses received, 60% of the parents have kids currently enrolled in preschool and around 20% whose kids previously attended preschool. 

More than half of the parents have responded stating that they have a weekly connect with the school regarding their child. According to the respondents scheduled parent-teacher meetup and almanac are the popular modes of communication about the activities of their child.

In an emergency majority of the parents were informed through phone call or emails and 40% parents were satisfied with this mode of communication.
