Jigsaw puzzle game

Sketches were made initially to get an idea of the whole website on paper and repititive feedback were taken from the end users and these feedbacks were implemented to give a user friendly Tablet Application

An academic project in the subject Rapid Prototyping, the design patterns were used and jigsaw puzzle game was created. Balsamiq was used to design the screens and later implemented in Axure. Different methods from sketching, paper prototyping, creating screens on InkScape and powerpoint, balsamiq and finally on Axure.
